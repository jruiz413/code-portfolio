    Jocelyn Ruiz is an art student currently enrolled at the University of Illinois at Chicago. She usually makes art through traditional drawing and painting but has experimented with many other art forms. Most recently, she has delved into creative coding as presented in this portfolio. Jocelyn Ruiz has created works that focus greatly on use of colors. Through her interactive pieces, she aimed to make something that satisifies and even calms the viewer.
