About

BTS is a seven member Korean group that debuted back in 2013 under a small music company. Over the years they've continued to rise in popuarity partly due the the huge amount of online content they show to their fans. They work tirelessly on their music bringing moving lyrics, complex coreography, and insane performances. At the beginning and even now they were talked down upon and told they were doomed to fail along with their company. Today they're continuing to win countless awards, break records, perform all around the world, and even spoke at the United Nations twice due to their impact. However the drawing thing about BTS is despite all the fame and money they recieve they continue to remain humble, not letting any of it get to their heads.

Their Music

Having been around for seven years, they've racked up quite a diverse discography. Many see them as just a pop group but Bts has experimented with many genres such as hip-hop, rap-rock, edm, punk, ballads, latin-pop, funk-rap, R&B, etc. Also, many assume every song includes all the members but they also release solo and unit songs in their albums. They are also very involved in creating their music as many of them members compose the music and write lyrics. The thing that makes them really stand out though are the lyrics and messages they make with their songs. BTS since debut has talked about issues and topics in their music that wasn't really being addressed in pop culture. They've tackled things like mental health, bullying, stress, politics, societal expecations and so much more. Recently their main message as been to love yourself and they've even launched campaigns about it.
